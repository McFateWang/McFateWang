WELCOME TO JIANXING CPR 2018

 CPR is a ZJUT event on postgraduate recommendation that brings together academics, individuals from JianXing college and other institutions to exchange information and ideas on the advancement of scientific research and postgraduate recommendation.

 The conference will feature a comprehensive program offering information on how to write and publish SCI/SSCI papers from the perspective of authors and reviewers via introducing proper papers showcasing the latest technologies and professional skills.

 In addition, the conference program includes keynote speeches and a panel discussion from senior schoolmate who have successfully obtained the qualifications of higher degrees.

 The 2018 edition of this conference will be held in ZJUT, 14th of November.

KEYNOTE CHAIRS

陈德仿，男，浙江温州人。浙江工业大学健行学院15级本科生。
